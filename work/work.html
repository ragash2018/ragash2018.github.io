Lakme
Pamper your Nails

Interaction Design

Creative Brief
To create an interactive print advertisement for Lakme Nail Polish.
Target Audience:
Urban Women who are well to do and are very conscious about their skin and nails. Women who do not want to waste their time in the store.
Research & Analysis
Lakmé is an Indian brand of cosmetics, owned by Unilever and run by CEO Anil Chopra. . Indian cosmetics Lakme was started in 1952, famously because the then Prime Minister, Jawaharlal Nehru, was concerned that Indian women were spending precious foreign exchange on beauty products, and personally requested JRD Tata to manufacture them in India. Lakme' has been ranked as 47th most trusted brand in India by The Brand Trust Report.
Parent company: HLL

Lakme has always advertised in the various mass media available. It also has a very good distribution network. They are placed only in large and reputed retail stores. The brand uses Celebrity Kareena Kapoor in the Tv commercials as well as in print ads.
Advantages of Lakme Nail Polish
Long-lasting, chip resistant nail enamel.
Contains resins to strengthen nails and prevents breakage.
Has colour lock technology.
Gives lacquer-like finish.
Has a variety of shades.
Concept
To make women choose their favourite nail polish shades without using the existing testers. I am introducing new sets of testers in the form of pre painted duplicate nails. The nails will be stuck on the print ad board and the women just have to put one of their fingers inside to have a look if that particular shade looks good on their nails. The name of that shade will glow as the finger will touch the board.

Many women do not like the idea of putting the tester colour actually on their nails before buying nail polish. There are hundreds of shades and it becomes very difficult to apply the tester colours one by one before women jump into conclusion which one to buy.

So my new interactive ad will make choosing shades easy for women and the poster will also get great attention as it requires women to actually come near the poster and try putting their fingers inside to finally pick the suitable one.
Concept Development
Elements of Design
Colours that will blend well with the nail polish ad.
Typeface that will closely match the one used on the product.
Lakme Logo.
Color Crush nail paint bottle.
Nail paint splash that gives an attractive effect to the poster.
The texts are alligned left and the graphic is on the extreme right.
The interaction design is below where the rectangle is placed.
COLOURS
Pink, purple and black.
Pink and purple are vibrant and girly colours.
Black has been used below on the background because it makes hands look prettier when placed on it.

TYPEFACE
Champagne & Limousines: This typeface is close to the one used on the color crush bottle.
INTERACTION
I am introducing new sets of testers in the form of pre painted duplicate nails. The nails will be stuck on the print ad board and the women just have to put one of their fingers inside to have a look if that particular shade looks good on their nails. The name of that shade will glow as the finger will touch the board.
MATERIALS USED
Acrylic sheets
Painted Duplicate nails
EDs
Touch switches
Batteries

Creative brief
To create a bookmark that enhances reading experience
Target Audience
Students of 14 years and above, who love reading anywhere and everywhere Brand Chosen: ITC Classmate
Research & Analysis
Classmate is the lead provider of all student stationery needs. ITC launched its Classmate brand in 2003 with the notebooks category. Subsequently, Classmate added new products to its portfolio which now consists of pens, pencils, mechanical pencils and geometry boxes, erasers and sharpeners.
Concept
This bookmark is designed keeping in mind the activities people do while reading. That is why a notepad and a page number setter have been introduced. People don't have to search for different items as mentioned above when they sit to read. This bookmark does it all.


Concept Development
It is a 6 cm wide and 15 cm high foldable bookmark. The bookmark also acts as a notepad where one can paste sticky notes while reading. The bookmark has page no. setter at the top with which one can set.
Plastic no. setter (scroller).
Bookmarks can usually have vast numbers of design and colours.
I have chosen one simple design as a sample.
Blue background with illustrations of white wheels and curve.

Prospectus Design

BRIEF
To redesign the NICC prospectus for the next academic year (2013-2014). The contents have been taken from the current prospectus and have been edited a little.
OBJECTIVE
To redesign the NICC prospectus using texts, graphic designs, photographs and colours. The priority is definitely readability.
DESIGN SYNOPSIS
Page size and orientation: 27.5cm*24 cm
The page size has been chosen so that it remains handy and the graphics and the texts do not clash with each other.
Layout: 3 column format.
Graphic Design: Coloured bars and Strokes
Colours: Tints of green, orange, violet and purple

Font: Since ARIAL is considered as the most readable font in the font families, therefore I preferred using ARIAL. It enhances readability.
Cover page:The theme of the prospectus becomes dominant from the cover page itself. Thick coloured bar and strokes along with few pictures have been used which give an overview of the college.
INSPIRATION
Design element: Coloured bars and strokes. I got the inspiration of using paint strokes from our fine arts class.
SUMMARY
The prospectus looks clean with harmonious colours and all the elements blend well.
The texts look readable and therefore it will draw attention of the target audience.
The important informations have been highlighted with different colours and font sizes.
